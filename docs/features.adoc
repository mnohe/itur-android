= User-visible features
:toc: left
:toclevels: 2
:icons: font

== Feature 1: Participate in an activity

Joining, being part of, and leaving a group activity.
This is the primary user journey; all other features support or extend it.

=== Join

. From the Idle state, tap *Join an activity*.
. System requests `CAMERA` permission if not yet granted.
. Grant the permission.
+
NOTE: If camera permission is denied, a toast informs the user that QR scanning requires camera access and the scan sheet is not opened.
Tapping *Join an activity* again repeats the toast until permission is granted.
. QR scan bottom sheet appears with live camera preview.
. Point camera at a valid Itur QR code.
. ML Kit decodes the QR; `IturActivityId` is extracted from the URL.
. Loading indicator appears.
. User is added as a participant in Firestore.
. Ongoing overlay appears with *Hail organiser*, *Exit activity*, *Follow self*, and *Follow group* FABs.

Location updates begin automatically; the device's position is published to the activity and participant markers appear on the map in real time.

=== Exit

. From the Ongoing state (participant), tap *Exit activity*.
. Location updates stop.
. User is removed from the participant list (the activity continues for others).
. Location data for this user is cleared.
. Idle overlay appears.

=== Auto-resume

If the app is closed or crashes while the user is a participant in an ongoing activity:

. Re-launch the app.
. `MapViewModel.init` queries for activities where the current user is listed as a participant with `ONGOING` status.
. If found: Ongoing overlay appears automatically without user interaction.
. If the activity has since ended: Idle overlay appears silently, with no message.
. If an error prevents rejoining: a dialog appears with *Retry* and *Exit activity* choices.

== Feature 2: Account

Authenticating with a Google account to unlock organiser capabilities, and returning to an anonymous session.

=== Sign in

. From the Idle state, tap *Sign in*.
. Google OAuth sheet appears (OS-managed Credential Manager).
. Select a Google account.
. App receives the ID token; Firebase Auth session is established.
. Sign-in FAB is replaced by a user info FAB and a sign-out FAB (light orange background).

=== Sign out

. From the Idle state (signed in), tap the *Sign out* FAB (light orange background).
+
NOTE: The sign-out FAB is hidden while an activity is ongoing; it reappears once the user returns to the Idle state.
. Firebase Auth session is cleared.
. App reverts to an anonymous device-UUID identity.
. Sign-out and user info FABs disappear; the Sign-in FAB reappears.

== Feature 3: Organise an activity

Creating, running, and stopping a group activity as the organiser.

=== Start

Any user in the Idle state can tap *Start an activity*.

==== Anonymous user

. Tap *Start an activity*.
. A dialog appears: "Starting an activity requires signing in."
. The dialog offers *Cancel* and *Sign in* choices.
. Tapping *Sign in* proceeds to the Google OAuth flow (Feature 2), then creates the activity.
. Tapping *Cancel* dismisses the dialog; app remains in the Idle state.

==== Registered user

. Tap *Start an activity*.
. Loading indicator appears (`Preparing activity…`).
. A new activity document is created in Firestore.
. Ongoing overlay appears; user is the organiser.
. *Show QR*, *Stop activity*, *Follow self*, *Follow group*, and *Get help* FABs are visible.
+
NOTE: The sign-out FAB is hidden in the Ongoing state.

Location updates begin automatically; the device's position is published to the activity and all participant markers are shown on the map in real time.

=== Share join QR

. From the Ongoing state (organiser), tap *Show QR*.
. QR display bottom sheet appears.
. QR code encodes `https://itur.cat/activity/{activityId}`.
. Other devices can scan this code to join.
. Tap outside the sheet or drag down to dismiss.

=== Stop

. From the Ongoing state (organiser), tap *Stop activity* (light orange background).
. Location updates stop.
. Activity status is updated to `FINISHED` in Firestore.
. User is removed from the participant list.
. Location data for the activity is cleared.
. Idle overlay appears.

=== Auto-resume

If the app is closed or crashes while the user has an activity they organised still ongoing:

. Re-launch the app.
. `MapViewModel.init` queries for activities where `organizerId == currentUser`.
. If found: Ongoing overlay appears automatically without user interaction.
. If the activity has since ended: Idle overlay appears silently, with no message.
. If an error prevents rejoining: a dialog appears with *Retry* and *Exit activity* choices.

== Feature 4: Follow self

A user in the Ongoing state can centre the map on their own location.

. Tap *Follow self* (Recenter) FAB.
. Map camera animates to the device's last known location.

== Feature 5: Follow group

A user in the Ongoing state can fit all participants in the map viewport.

. Tap *Follow group* (Track group) FAB.
. Map camera animates to a bounding box that contains all participant locations.
. As subsequent location updates arrive, the bounding box automatically expands, shrinks, or repositions to keep all participants in view.

== Feature 6: Hail organiser

An optional additional feature available to participants during an ongoing activity.
The app remains fully functional without it.

=== Participant side

. From the Ongoing state (participant), tap *Hail organiser*.
. The FAB changes colour to indicate the active hailing state.
. An attention request is sent to Firestore.
. To cancel the hail, tap *Hail organiser* again — the FAB returns to its default colour.
. If the organiser acknowledges the hail, the participant's FAB is automatically restored to its default colour.

=== Organiser side

. An in-app notification appears identifying the hailing participant(s).
. The notification can be expanded to list all currently hailing participants.
. Each entry has an *Acknowledge* button; tapping it removes that participant from the list and restores their hail button.
. An *Acknowledge all* option clears the entire list at once.
